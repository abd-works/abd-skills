# Grill me — abd-ux-user-impact-map

**Mechanics:** [`common/reference/grill-me-with-practice-skill.md`](../../../../common/reference/grill-me-with-practice-skill.md) — one question at a time; generate-to-learn when enough is shared.

Ask until the map at this fidelity level is unambiguous:

- Is the top-level goal a measurable business outcome, or a feature already decided?
- Who can block or undermine this goal that is not listed as an actor?
- Are impacts observable behaviour changes in the actor, or team tasks?
- Does each deliverable serve exactly one impact, or bundle unrelated work?
- Which build or outcome assumption would kill the initiative if wrong — and is it stated on the map?
