# Grill me — abd-ux-specification

**Mechanics:** [`common/reference/grill-me-with-practice-skill.md`](../../../../common/reference/grill-me-with-practice-skill.md) — one question at a time; generate-to-learn when enough is shared.

Ask until the prototype at this fidelity level is unambiguous:

- Can a stakeholder walk the happy path without explanation — or does someone need to narrate?
- Where does the prototype stop and imagination begin — can stakeholders tell stubbed from real?
- What does the user see on failure — network error, invalid input, expired session?
- Are typography, colour, and spacing final or placeholder — will stakeholders approve stand-ins?
- Can every acceptance criterion be completed end-to-end — or are there dead ends?
- Do stakeholders understand they are approving interaction and visual direction, not production code?
