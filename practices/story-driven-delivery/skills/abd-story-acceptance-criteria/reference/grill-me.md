# Grill me — abd-story-acceptance-criteria

**Mechanics:** [`common/grill-me-with-practice-skill.md`](../../../../common/grill-me-with-practice-skill.md) — one question at a time; generate-to-learn when enough is shared.

Ask until "done" is unambiguous for each story:

- Who actually triggers this behaviour — is "the user" hiding different actors with different definitions of done?
- Is this one observable interaction, or several behaviours in one story? Can you state done in 4–9 criteria?
- What must **not** happen — rejections, timeouts, conflicts, unauthorised access?
- Do these terms match domain language, or has the team invented synonyms?
- Can a stakeholder verify each criterion from observable behaviour, or do some describe internal state only?
