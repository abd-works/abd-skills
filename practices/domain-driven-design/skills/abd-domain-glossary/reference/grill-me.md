# Grill me — abd-domain-glossary

**Mechanics:** [`common/reference/grill-me-with-practice-skill.md`](../../../../common/reference/grill-me-with-practice-skill.md) — one question at a time; generate-to-learn when enough is shared.

- Which terms mean different things to different people — where is the collision?
- What concepts does everyone use in conversation but nobody has named?
- What is being treated as a standalone concept that is really an attribute (or vice versa)?
- Are module groupings driven by shared concern or by document co-location?
- For boundary terms: who owns them — is that agreed or assumed?
