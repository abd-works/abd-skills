# Grill me — abd-ddd-design-building-blocks

**Mechanics:** [`common/reference/grill-me-with-practice-skill.md`](../../../../../common/reference/grill-me-with-practice-skill.md) — one question at a time; generate-to-learn when enough is shared.

- Does this concept truly need persistent identity, or is that habit?
- What invariant does this aggregate protect — can you state it as a business rule?
- Are any "entities" actually interchangeable by value with no lifecycle?
- Is this domain service genuinely homeless, or does the operation belong on an entity?
- Does the business need immediate consistency here — who decided?
