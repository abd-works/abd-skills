# Grill me — abd-domain-model

**Mechanics:** [`common/reference/grill-me-with-practice-skill.md`](../../../../common/reference/grill-me-with-practice-skill.md) — one question at a time; generate-to-learn when enough is shared.

- When two concepts could own the same behavior, which one actually does — what's the real-world evidence?
- Which business rules feel obvious but haven't been stated — what breaks if they're missing from the model?
- When two concepts interact, which one initiates — does that match how the business works?
- When behavior varies by kind, is it structural variation or just a flag?
- Are there behaviors assigned to existing concepts that belong on a concept nobody has named yet?
