# Team agenda — Context-driven delivery, skills & agents

**Suggested subject line:** Workshop agenda — mental models, AI skills, agentic workflows (full day)

---

Hi team,

Here’s a short overview of the upcoming offsite agenda—looking forward to seeing you there.

## Purpose

This day is for **ABD people to skill up** and so ew  **new ways of working**—not a tour of buzzwords. We want everyone leaving with **shared language**, **hands-on practice** building reusable skills from real delivery work, and enough exposure to **agents** that we can talk about how work actually flows when AI is part of the team. Context-Driven Delivery is the **frame**; the **point** is that we leave more capable and aligned on how we want to work together.

---

## At a glance


| Time                  | Session                 | What we’ll do                                                                                                                                                           |
| --------------------- | ----------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **9:00–10:30**        | Shared mental modelling | Align on purpose; intro to Context-Driven Delivery; surface concerns; sketch an end-to-end workflow; spot modular levels for low-latency AI; intro to an **AI charter** |

| **After / follow-on** | Agents & delivery       | Themes on context-driven practice, agentic trade-offs, and optimization—**exact agenda to be confirmed**                                                                |
