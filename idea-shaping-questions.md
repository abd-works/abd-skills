# Idea Shaping — Questions & Facts Uncovered

---

## Opportunity Canvas
Shape a new idea before committing to build.

**Questions**
- Who hurts?
- What's broken?
- Who pays?
- Worth building?
- What's assumed?
- Partners needed?
- Costs what?
- Ships when?

**Facts**
- Customer segments and their needs
- The value proposition
- Smallest first release
- How we'll know it worked
- Cost structure
- Key dependencies
- Named assumptions

---

## Impact Mapping
Connect org goals to behaviour change to scope.
Shape a new idea before committing to build.

**Questions**
- Why bother?
- Who changes behaviour?
- Changes how?
- Build what?
- Orphaned features?
- Cross-actor conflicts?

**Facts**
- Business outcome
- Observable behaviour changes
- Candidate things to build
- Goal proof metrics
- "If we build X, actor will do Y" bets
- "If Y happens, goal moves" bets

## Simple Validated Learning
Test beliefs before building.

**Questions**
- Believe what?
- Could be wrong?
- Smallest test?
- Who owns it?
- By when?
- Blind spots?

**Facts**
- Ranked assumptions
- Testable belief statements
- Test methods and timeboxes
- What counts as failure
- What to build vs what to discover first
- What decision follows each test

---

## Cost of Delay
Order work by economic impact of waiting.

**Questions**
- How does this create value?
- How fast does value fade?
- What does waiting cost per week?
- Does order matter?
- Which numbers are guesses?

**Facts**
- Weekly cost of not having this in market
- Value-per-week divided by delivery time (scheduling score)
- Which assumptions could change the numbers
- Dollar gap between best and worst ordering
- Why smaller batches score higher
